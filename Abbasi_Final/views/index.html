Welcome to your luxurious home away from home

When searching for a glamping destination in Atlantic Canada, one is immediately captivated by the region's natural beauty and diverse landscapes. From rugged coastlines to pristine forests, Atlantic Canada offers a wealth of options for those seeking a luxurious camping experience. The allure of waking up to the sound of waves crashing on the shore or birds chirping in the wilderness adds a unique charm to glamping in this region. Whether one seeks a waterfront yurt in Nova Scotia, or a secluded cabin with stunning views in New Brunswick, Atlantic Canada promises an unforgettable and immersive connection with nature. Your Accomodations

A space for everyone. The Family Suite

Glamping with the entire family in a yurt is an extraordinary and memorable experience that fosters a sense of togetherness and adventure. The circular canvas structure, reminiscent of ancient nomadic dwellings, provides a spacious and comfortable setting for all family members to enjoy. Nestled amidst nature's embrace, the yurt offers an escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life, allowing the family to reconnect and create lasting memories. Inside, the cozy furnishings and rustic charm offer a perfect balance between modern comfort and the allure of the great outdoors. From storytelling around a crackling campfire to stargazing through the skylight above, the yurt provides the ideal backdrop for family bonding. Whether exploring nearby trails, engaging in outdoor activities, or simply relishing the tranquility, glamping in a yurt with the entire family guarantees an unforgettable and enriching journey into the heart of nature.
